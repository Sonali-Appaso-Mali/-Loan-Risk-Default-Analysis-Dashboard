
# Business Insights

The dashboard provides valuable insights into lending performance and financial risk.

## Key Insights

- Home loans contribute the largest share of the total loan amount.
- Default rates vary across employment categories.
- Full-time employees generally have higher average incomes.
- Adult applicants receive higher average loan amounts.
- Loan performance changes over different years.
- Borrower demographics influence lending patterns.
- Credit score categories show different loan distributions.
- High-income borrowers contribute significantly to the total loan portfolio.
